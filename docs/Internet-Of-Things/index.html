Courses on Internet of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the interconnected network of physical devices that communicate and exchange data over the internet. IoT is revolutionizing various industries by enabling smart devices to collect, analyze, and act on data.

At Campus Connect, we offer a range of IoT courses that cover the fundamentals, applications, and security aspects of IoT technologies.
